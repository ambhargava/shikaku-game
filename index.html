How to Play

📝 Rule 1: Match the Numbers

Each number tells you the area (number of cells) of its rectangle.

3

= rectangle with 3 cells

🎯 Rule 2: One Number Per Rectangle

Each rectangle must contain exactly one number.

2
2

❌ Wrong: 2 numbers

🧩 Rule 3: Cover Everything

Fill the entire grid with rectangles. No gaps allowed! Rectangles cannot overlap.

💡 Tip: Drag across cells to create rectangles. Tap a colored cell to delete and start over.
